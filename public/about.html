SriKrishna Real Estate

Our Purpose

"We are dedicated to creating landmarks that inspire generations."

Founded in 1980, SriKrishna Real Estate has grown from a single residential project into one of the region's most trusted real estate developers. Our journey is one of unwavering commitment — to quality, to community, and to creating spaces that stand the test of time.

The SriKrishna Legacy

From One Dream to a Thousand Skylines

What began as a vision to bring well-built, honestly-priced homes to families has evolved into a real estate business trusted across the region. Under a leadership team dedicated to quality and transparency, we have delivered millions of square feet of premium residential and commercial real estate.

Today, SriKrishna Real Estate operates across multiple cities — with each development setting new benchmarks for design, construction quality, and sustainable living.

We believe every home should be a source of pride and every development a contribution to the community it belongs to.
